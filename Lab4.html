Grilled Cheese Bacon Tomato Sandwich Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Cheese,
  • Bacon,
  • Tomato,
  • Sandwich bread,
  • Butter,

Directions:

First take your butter and fry your bacon until it is crispy on a pan. Then slice your tomato in to disks. Then take 2 pieces of bread and put some american cheese between it. Then take your cheese sandwich and put on the frying pan and cook with butter on both sides. When both sides are golden brown and the cheese is melting, take the sandwich apart and add the bacon and tomato.
